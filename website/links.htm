Professional Affiliations

British Horoligical Institute (BHI)

The BHI are the professional body for Horologists in the UK. Being a qualified Member requires the adherence to the BHI Code of Practice. They support their members with training, a monthly magazine etc.
www.bhi.co.uk

British Watch and Clockmakers Guild

Membership of The British Watch and Clock Makers' Guild is by election and is available to those who are professionally engaged in any branch of horology and allied crafts.
www.bwcmg.org

The Chronometer Club

A great technical forum for professional horologists focusing on the servicing of chronometer watches.

I am member, and former committee member of the BHI South West Branch. They host monthly presentations from a wide range of speakers, and hold an annual auction.
